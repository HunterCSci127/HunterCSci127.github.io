CSci 127: Introduction to Computer Science
Department of Computer Science
Hunter College, City University of New York
Spring 2025

TL;DR: A first course in computer science, primarily in Python. See the FAQ and resources pages for more details.

Announcements:

  • Test-outs: If you know the material for this course (see outline and past final exams below), the department offers credit by passing the test-out exam. For more details, including the upcoming test-out dates in January, see the credit by exam on the department website.
  • Weekly Lecture: Tuesdays 10-11:15am, in 118HN, start on January 28.
  • Weekly Lab, Quiz & Code Review: In 1001G, self-scheduled appointments via Navigate or drop-in tutoring available.
  • Tutoring: Peer-mentoring is available 11:30am-5pm weekdays when classes are in session in 1001G. The lab opens for the semester on Thursday, 30 January.
